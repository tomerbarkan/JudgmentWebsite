Help Judgment achieve it's full potential

Survivors,

We hope that your being here means that you enjoy Judgment, and you want to help us make the best game we can. Thanks!

The more people that play and enjoy the game, the more time and money we can dedicade to support it post-release. Here are a few ways in which you can help get Judgment in front of more people.

Write a Steam review

Let others know what you like about the game. This is very important to us.

Add user-defined tags on Steam

Select any tags that you believe accurately describe the game on our store page. The more the better. This helps Steam recommend the game to the correct audience.

Spread the word

Share on Facebook and Twitter. Post on reddit or other game forums. Let others know about Judgment.

Content creators

Contact your favorite Youtubers, Streamers and Magazines. Tweet to them, write them an email, or comment on their videos. Tell them about Judgment and ask them to cover the game. We will gladly send them a press key.

Steam curators

Contact your favorite Steam Curators and tell them about Judgment. Ask them to curate the game. We will gladly send them a press key.

Send us feedback

Ideas? Suggestions? Complaints? Please let us know what you think. We try our best to listen and improve.

Thank you for all your support! Together, we will drive the demons away.
