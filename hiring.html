Hiring - Game Designer & Marketeer

Suncrash is searching for a game designer & marketeer for PC title Judgment: Apocalypse Survival Simulation.

You'll get a chance to work on a cool survival/management game with demons in it, and work with a great team (we're not demons though). We're already out in Steam Early Access where players enjoy the game.

You'll work with the team to design and promote the game. We're looking for designers who are passionate and are very familiar with hardcore PC strategy, simulation and/or management games.

Your Role

  • Design new content, mechanics and systems for the game.
  • Collect, organize and decide how to use player feedback.
  • Create marketing materials such as gifs, screenshots, and articles.
  • Plan and execute creative ways to promote the game.

    • Requirements

  • Very familiar with and passionate about PC strategy games - must.
  • Ability to analyze, and discuss games from a critical perspective.
  • Creative and independent.
  • Autodidact.
  • Fluent in English.
  • Previous experience is a plus.
  • Relevant formal education is a plus.

    • If you're interested and meet the requirements - please contact us at contact@suncrash.com and let us know why you think you would be good for the job.
